My Artistic Journey

Since I was young, I loved anything that had to do with creating things. I did different types of art, from drawing and painting to DIY crafts. I have created many things, from paintings with acrylic paint to painting my own house and building my own closet. You could say I have tried different mediums of art, which allowed me to find my love for drawing and painting.

This is what sparked my interest in tattooing, but it led me to fall in love with another form of art. Giving someone a beautiful art piece they want that will last practically forever, really brings a lot of joy in my life.

Why tattoos?

Tattoos can be memories, milestones or part of one's identity. Tattoos can carry a deep meaning to someone or it could just be a form of artistic expression, simply an image that they enjoy. Regardless, it will be on one's body for a lifetime.

I'm not saying that other mediums of art won't last, but carrying it on your body is truly a special type of expression. Tattoos have so much meaning just from the memory of it, marking a time in someone's life when they were adding to their identity. If the image is meaningful to the individual, it's even more special! I love being a part of creating something special and unique for each client.
