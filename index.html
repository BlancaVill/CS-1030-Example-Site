My Artistic Journy

Since I was young, I loved anything that had to do with creating things. I did different types of art starting with drawing to painting to DIY crafts. I have created many things from paintings with acrylic paint to painting my own house and building my own closet. You can say I have tried different mediums of art also, allowing me to find my love for drawing/painting.

This is what sparked my interest in tattooing, but it led me to falling in love with another form of art. Giving someone a beautiful art piece they wanted that will last practically forever, really brings a lot of joy in my life.

Why tattoos?

Tattoos can be memories, milestones or part of one's identity. A tattoos can carry so much meaning to someone or it could just be a expression of art for them, simply just an image that they enjoy, but it will be on one's body for a lifetime. Not saying that other mediums of art won't last, but carrying it on your body is defintely a special type of art form. With that being said, I believe that a tattoo has so much meaning just from the memory of it. It is a time in someone life when they were adding to thier identity, regarless of the image. If the image is meaningful to the individual, it's even more special! I love being apart of creating something special and unique for each client.
